Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our photography services, booking process, and more. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

What types of photography services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of photography services including:

  • Wedding photography and videography
  • Pre-wedding shoots
  • Portrait photography (individual, family, maternity)
  • Event photography (corporate events, birthdays, anniversaries)
  • Commercial and product photography
  • Drone photography and videography

How far in advance should I book my photography session?

We recommend booking as early as possible to ensure availability, especially for weddings and events during peak season (typically October to March).

  • Weddings: 6-12 months in advance
  • Portrait sessions: 2-4 weeks in advance
  • Events: 1-3 months in advance

Last-minute bookings are subject to availability.

What is included in your photography packages?

Our packages vary based on the type of photography service, but typically include:

  • Professional photography coverage
  • High-resolution edited digital images
  • Online gallery for viewing and sharing
  • Printing rights
  • Optional add-ons: albums, prints, additional hours, second photographer

We offer customized packages to meet your specific needs and budget.

How long does it take to receive our photos?

Our delivery time depends on the type and scale of the project:

  • Portrait sessions: 2-3 weeks
  • Events: 3-4 weeks
  • Weddings: 4-8 weeks

We always provide a few preview images within 48 hours for you to share on social media.

Do you travel for photography assignments?

Yes, we travel for photography assignments throughout India and internationally. Travel fees may apply for locations outside Mumbai, which include transportation, accommodation, and incidental expenses. We offer customized quotes for destination photography.

Can I request specific shots or poses?

Absolutely! We encourage clients to share their ideas, must-have shots, and inspiration images before the session. During our pre-shoot consultation, we'll discuss your vision and preferences to ensure we capture exactly what you're looking for.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that circumstances change. Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • More than 30 days notice: Full refund of deposit
  • 15-30 days notice: 50% refund of deposit
  • Less than 15 days notice: Deposit is non-refundable

In case of rescheduling, we'll do our best to accommodate your new date based on availability.

Do you provide raw, unedited images?

We do not typically provide raw images as they are unfinished products that don't represent our quality of work. Our creative process includes careful selection and professional editing to deliver images that meet our standards. The final edited images you receive will be of the highest quality.

Still have questions? Contact us and we'll be happy to help!
