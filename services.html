Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our photography services

How far in advance should we book your services?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for weddings and 2-4 weeks for other sessions. Popular dates (especially wedding seasons) can book up a year in advance, so early booking is encouraged.

Do you travel for photoshoots?

Yes, we travel throughout India for photoshoots. Travel expenses beyond 50km from Mumbai are billed separately. International destination shoots are also available with custom pricing.

How long until we receive our photos?

Our standard delivery time is 3-4 weeks for regular sessions and 6-8 weeks for weddings. We always provide a few preview images within 48 hours of your session for sharing on social media.

Can we request specific shots or poses?

Absolutely! We encourage clients to share inspiration images, shot lists, or specific requests before the session. During our pre-shoot consultation, we'll discuss all your ideas and incorporate them into our plan.

What happens if the weather is bad on our shoot day?

For outdoor shoots, we monitor the weather closely. If conditions are unfavorable, we'll work with you to reschedule at no additional cost. We also have studio options available as backup.
