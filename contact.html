Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How far in advance should I book my photography session?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for portrait sessions and 6-12 months in advance for weddings. Popular dates fill up quickly, especially during wedding season (October to March).

What is included in your photography packages?

Our packages typically include photography coverage, high-resolution edited digital images, online gallery access, and printing rights. Additional services like albums, prints, and extra hours can be added to customize your package.

Do you travel for photoshoots?

Yes, we travel throughout Mumbai and surrounding areas. Destination photography is also available with travel expenses calculated based on location. Contact us for a custom quote for destination shoots.

How long does it take to receive our photos?

Typically, portrait sessions are delivered within 2-3 weeks, and weddings within 6-8 weeks. We always provide a few preview images within 48 hours for you to share on social media.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require a 50% deposit to secure your booking. Cancellations made more than 30 days before the session will receive a full refund of the deposit. Within 30 days, the deposit is non-refundable but can be applied to a future session within 6 months.
